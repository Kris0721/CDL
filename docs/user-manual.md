# User Manual - DeFi Lending Platform

## Getting Started

### For Borrowers

#### 1. Registration
1. Visit the platform website
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Fill in your details (name, email, password)
4. Select "Borrower" as account type
5. Verify your email with the OTP sent

#### 2. Connect Wallet
1. Click "Connect Wallet" in the top right
2. Select MetaMask or your preferred wallet
3. Approve the connection

#### 3. Request a Loan
1. Navigate to "Request Loan"
2. Enter loan amount (in USDC)
3. Select loan duration (30, 60, 90, or 180 days)
4. Describe the purpose of the loan
5. Submit the request
6. Wait for admin approval

#### 4. Receive Funds
Once approved, funds will be automatically transferred to your connected wallet.

#### 5. Repay Loan
1. Navigate to "Repay Loan"
2. Select the loan to repay
3. Enter repayment amount
4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet

### For Lenders

#### 1. Registration
1. Visit the platform website
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Fill in your details
4. Select "Lender" as account type
5. Verify your email

#### 2. Deposit Funds
1. Navigate to "Lend Funds"
2. Enter the amount to lend
3. Select lock duration
4. Review estimated returns
5. Approve the transaction in your wallet

#### 3. Track Earnings
1. Navigate to "Profit History"
2. View all interest payments
3. See total earnings

#### 4. Withdraw Funds
After the lock period ends, you can withdraw your principal plus interest.

### For Maintainers

#### 1. User Management
- View all registered users
- Approve/reject KYC documents
- Manage user status

#### 2. Loan Management
- Review loan requests
- Approve/reject loans
- Monitor active loans

#### 3. System Monitoring
- View transaction logs
- Monitor system health
- Track platform metrics

## FAQ

### How do I calculate my returns?
Returns = Principal × (APR / 100) × (Days / 365)

### When can I withdraw my funds?
You can withdraw after the lock period ends.

### What happens if I miss a payment?
Late payments may affect your credit score and future loan eligibility.

### Is my money safe?
All funds are secured by smart contracts on the Ethereum blockchain.

### What tokens are supported?
Currently, we support USDC for all transactions.
